QUE MONO
Edited and designed by Max Kessel.
Text by Clara Rojas.
Artist book and thesis
This thesis explores the realm of memories, symbols and how they are affected by the pass of time. Specifically, I look at my ongoing relationship with images, objects and imaginaries that belong to my childhood. These items share notions of cute-ness and behold qualities associated with the notion of “childish”. In my life I have extended the relationship with these materials and (objects,content..)imaginaries beyond their preassigned calendar
